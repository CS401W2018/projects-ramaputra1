What is Slank?

Slank is a very famous rock band from Indonesia, formed in 1983 in Jakarta. Their music mixes rock, blues, and traditional Indonesian sounds. Many of their songs talk about social and political issues, which helped them gain a lot of fans. Slank is also known for their strong anti-drug message after some members recovered from addiction. They are very influential in Indonesia's culture and music.
