What is Ramen Ichiraku?

Ramen Ichiraku is a fictional ramen shop from the anime and manga series Naruto.
It's a favorite place for Naruto Uzumaki, the main character, to eat ramen.
The shop is known for its friendly owner, Teuchi.

Ramen Ichiraku is based on a real ramen shop in Fukuoka, Japan, where Naruto creator Masashi Kishimoto used to eat.
Ramen Ichiraku has become an iconic symbol in the Naruto series.
I love it because the ramen always looks simple but so delicious, make everyone who see it want to get try!
