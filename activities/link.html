  2. Explain the difference between an absolute link and a relative link. Why would you use relative links?

    Absolute link: Direct to another different website.
    Relatives link: Only to your website only. Just like in english, relatives means your family (Father, Mother, Brother) so it means to your website. For example: The navigation, only move from one nav to another nav.

    We use relative link when we only need to navigate to another link in our own website (folder) for example: Navigation link.
